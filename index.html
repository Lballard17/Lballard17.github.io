Strengths

I have so many strengths like, I'm smart in certain areas, love to make people laugh, love to write, not a giver upper, I love to take on new callenges, I have a GREAT visual memory

Challenges

Having FAS has it's challenges.For me those challenges are impulsive control, social Q's, hard time staying focused for certain things, lying when I don't want to, acting out in certain ways.

Some obsticles to face havig FAS are things like, knowing your not going to be the same as everyone else, trying to figure out a way to fit in, trying to come up with other ways to handle certain things like impulsive control etc..
